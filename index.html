Helping Senior Citizens Find Their Snailmate

Find a new companion, exchange stories, and send care to senior citizens through snail mail

why snailmate?

Human connection is a powerful and fulfilling human need.

Snailmate is a tool for you to build meaningful relationships through handwritten conversations.

Studies have shown that intergenerational relationships can promote positive emotional and mental health. Above all else, it can provide a way to make a new friend.

We'll provide the envelope, stamp, and some stickers for your first letter. All you need is a conversation to get started.

how it works

1. CREATE AN ACCOUNT

Personalize your Snailmate profile and keep track of your penpal matches

2. FIND A SNAILMATE

View the profiles of senior citizens, see their interests, and pair up with a click

3. SEND YOUR LETTER

Write to your Snailmate. For inspo, explore the tips page for first-time penpals
